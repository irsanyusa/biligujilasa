Lindsey Stirling Gears Up for Solo Headlining Tour This Summer

August 2024 · 2 minute read

TALENTRECAP.COM Entertainment Celebrity

Photo by Steve Jennings/Getty Images

Reading Time: 2 minutes

Lindsey Stirling is ready to fill up her summer with tons of live shows around the country. The violin virtuoso is set to hit the road in multiple dates in August and September.

Lindsey Stirling Announces 2023 Summer Tour

Last Monday, March 20, 2023, Stirling happily announced on Instagram that she’s heading back on tour. Serving as her big-time summer engagement, the violinist will run across the United States to showcase her top-tier musical skills and enchanting acrobatics.

As she headlines the tour, the “Snow Waltz” creator will be joined by indie-pop collective Walk Off The Earth. Meanwhile, the show will kick off on August 1 in Bridgeport, Connecticut and will conclude on September 2 in Bend, Oregon.

Stirling will play two shows each in Virginia, Ohio, and North Carolina. Subsequently, she will travel to the Midwest to make stops in Indianapolis, Des Moines, and more. The violinist will also drop by Southaven, Mississippi; Lincoln, Nebraska; and Moorhead, Minnesota. She will then continue to make stops across until the summer tour ends in the first week of September.

Fans Are Excited to See Her Perform

Upon learning about the happy news, fans are quick to intimate their good words to Stirling. Her supporter obviously can’t to see and meet her during the summer tour.

“So excited to see you coming back to Bend!” one person wrote. “You had a killer show last year and rocked our small town world. Can’t wait to see you this summer!”

To those who want to avail tickets using early access options, pre-sale in different payment platforms will begin on March 21. General sale will start on March 24 via Ticketmaster.

According to Stirling, fans also have the choice to secure VIP Package deals. These include a summer picnic with the musician, a Q&A session with her, and fun games.
