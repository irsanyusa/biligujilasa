Selena Gomez Teases New Song 'Single Soon' After More Than a Year

May 2024 · 3 minute read

TALENTRECAP.COM Entertainment Celebrity

NBC via Getty Images

Reading Time: 3 minutes

The music world is buzzing with excitement as Selena Gomez, the multi-talented American singer and actress, confirms her long-awaited comeback. Gomez, known for her powerful vocals and compelling performances, took to her official Instagram account to reveal the exciting news that she’s making her return with a brand new song titled “Single Soon.”

Selena Gomez Teased New Upcoming Music

Gomez’s musical journey is famous for a string of hits and memorable albums. While fans eagerly await her forthcoming full album SG3, the starlet has decided to grace them with a playful and upbeat tune, perfect for bidding farewell to the sun-soaked days of summer.

“Single Soon” isn’t just another song; it’s a reflection of Gomez’s creative energy showing her commitment to connecting with her fans. The producer of the track is none other than Benny Blanco and Cashmere Cat, two renowned figures in the music production scene. Set for a release date next week on August 25, the announcement comes as a delightful surprise for her dedicated fanbase.

The anticipation surrounding Gomez’s comeback has been palpable. Last year, she offered a glimpse of her musical talents by lending her voice to Rema’s hit “Calm Down,” which soared to the top spot on Billboard’s Global chart.

Despite this success, fans have been eagerly waiting for a full-fledged Gomez project. The release of “Single Soon” is expected to satisfy that craving, giving fans a much-needed musical treat. With “Single Soon,” Gomez is poised to reclaim her spot in the musical limelight, reminding us all of the power of her voice and the emotions her music evokes.

Selena Gomez Launched a Campaign Where Fans Can Win Prizes

What’s more, Gomez has cleverly dropped a trail of breadcrumbs for her devoted fans to follow. The recently published website illbesinglesoon.com has caught the attention of eagle-eyed fans, adorned with the words “Single Soon?” in bold red letters.

The website unveiled a form that invites fans to nominate friends or loved ones who might be going through a breakup, heartbreak, or a dating dry spell. The idea behind it? To celebrate being single soon or single now with an all-inclusive night out that includes everything needed for a memorable evening.

The form provides participants with a chance to win one of five exciting prize packages, each curated to enhance the experience of the “Single Soon” theme. The prize packages include a “Rare Beauty” gift package, Lyft and GoPuff gift cards for a seamless night out, a Gomez merchandise gift package, and even a VISA gift card for added convenience.

To enter the contest, participants need to provide details about the nominee who deserves the “Single Soon” night out. This includes the nominee’s first and last names, as well as the nominator’s details, including their first and last names, city, and email address. Additionally, entrants need to share in 250 words or less why their nominee deserves this special evening on the town.
