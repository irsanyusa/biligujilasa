Charli DAmelio Slays With Landon Barker On a Photo Shoot For Mini V

July 2024 · 3 minute read

TALENTRECAP.COM Talent Dancing With The Stars

V Magazine

Reading Time: 3 minutes

Charli D’Amelio and her boyfriend Landon Barker have captured their love on the pages of Mini V magazine, in their first joint magazine cover shoot. The couple, who confirmed their relationship in 2022, posed for the cover and inside the spread.

Charli D’Amelio Poses With Boyfriend Landon Barker For Mini V

The magazine shoot captures the young couple’s love for each other and their passion for their respective careers. D’Amelio, who won the mirrorball trophy on the last season of Dancing with the Stars, dazzles in a bedazzled fringe dress while Barker rocks a dark, oversized suit and holds a guitar.

In one of the photos, the couple embrace each other as they pose in a dance studio, while in another, D’Amelio shows off her dance skills in a skirt and ballet flats, leaping in the air next to Barker.

The couple has been spotted together at various events such as the Grammys, Kids Choice Awards and DWTS. In an interview with ET in February, they reflected on their journey so far and how they have supported each other through the ups and downs of their careers.

For D’Amelio, it has been exciting to see Barker grow and evolve as an aspiring musician, while Barker has been supportive of D’Amelio’s journey on DWTS. During the interview portion of the shoot, they revealed some insights about their relationship, including when they were happiest, their greatest loves, and their idea of happiness.

The Couple Opens Up About Their Relationship

Being a teenager is already a roller coaster of emotions and experiences, but what happens when you add millions of followers and the public eye to the mix? D’Amelio and Barker are two of Gen Z’s most-watched celebrities, and can attest to the ups and downs of growing up in the public eye.

As teenagers, D’Amelio and Barker are still figuring out life, and they don’t have all the answers. But what is clear is their love for each other, their families, and their passions. They share their views on happiness, fears, personal traits they like and dislike, and the qualities they admire in others.

D’Amelio believes that perfect happiness comes from surrounding herself with the people she loves every day. Barker, on the other hand, doesn’t believe perfect happiness exists but envisions himself creating art surrounded by friends and family.

In conclusion, while it’s easy to see D’Amelio and Barker as the perfect couple with perfect lives, they are, in fact, just like any other teenager, trying to figure out who they are and where they belong. By sharing their candid and genuine responses, they remind us that it’s okay to be imperfect and that it’s what makes us unique and authentic.
