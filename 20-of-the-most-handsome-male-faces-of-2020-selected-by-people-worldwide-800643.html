20 of the Most Handsome Male Faces of 2020 Selected by People Worldwide

September 2024 · 1 minute read

Every year, the online entertainment portal, TC Candler, puts together a top 100 list of the most attractive men and women around the globe. They accept suggestions from millions of people to keep the rankings as diverse as possible. Last year’s top 3 consisted of Shawn Mendes at #3, Felix Kjellberg — also known as the YouTube star, PewDiePie — at #2, and Jungkook, a member of the K-pop band, BTS, at the top of the list. Let’s see who reigns supreme this time.
