September 2024 · 2 minute read

She made this jaw-dropping disclosure on her Instagram story on Monday, October 23, 2023, while trolling her father for smearing her name to family members and lying about her. It sure didn’t stop there because they hated me for telling my mum and went on to start bullying me in school and telling their friends to not befriend me and always pick on me so they can beat me. I went through all this as a teen."
