I Feel Like 30 Is Too Late, Kylie Jenner Admits That Being a Young Mom Is What She Always Want

June 2024 · 3 minute read

Some people may say that being a young mom is a tricky business. You have to balance motherhood, a career, and the joys of being young. But it seems like that’s not the case for Kylie Jenner. Being a mom of 2 at age 25, she is running her multimillion-dollar business with her face on the covers of famous magazines. However, the question of how she successfully handles all the different spheres of her life still remains a secret for many.

She always wanted to be a young mom.

Now a mother of 2, Kylie Jenner welcomed her first baby when she was only 20 years old. And despite different reactions from people, the star has shared that this is exactly what she wanted. Jenner was planning to become a young mom and motherhood is what she wanted to do. “Being a young mom just means we met a little early and I get to love you a little longer,” Jenner captioned her post devoted to her daughter.

Her father, Caitlyn Jenner (formerly known as Bruce Jenner), also shared that the young woman wanted to start a family very early on, and luckily, now she has all the necessary conditions to make sure that her children are well taken care of.

For Kylie Jenner, motherhood is an amazing experience.

The young mother, in an interview with her sister, Kim Kardashian, has admitted that even though for some women the early stages of motherhood are a challenge, for her, this period was much more fun. Of course, you can’t avoid certain problems, like sleepless nights and mood swings, but the overall experience brought the star more joy than challenges.

After giving birth to her first daughter, Stormi, Jenner shared that she thinks of her anywhere she is and would love to take her everywhere. Now as Stormi gets older, her mom has started to take her more often to public events so they can be together.

She is planning to have a private life.

Considering her own reality career at age 9, Jenner shared that she would rather raise her children away from lights and cameras. The star was planning to keep her family life private and live outside the city with her children. And although her popularity has grown in recent years, Jenner is still looking forward to living an ordinary life with her family and having a farm with chickens.

Motherhood made her more confident.

Jenner shared that before, she was feeling insecure, so she has undergone some cosmetic procedures to tackle them. However, after becoming a mom, she changed her mind about that. In one of her interviews, she admitted that she didn’t even like her ears but now she has a different opinion on her appearance that she wants to pass on to her daughter. Jenner is trying to love herself more and be an example to Stormi as a self-confident woman.

Despite all the joys, she didn’t feel well postpartum.

After welcoming her second child, a son that they initially named Wolf Webster, Jenner shared with the public that this postpartum experience was much harder than the one she had with her daughter. The star dished, “It’s not easy mentally, physically, spiritually, it’s just crazy.”

Jenner has shared that for all moms this experience is different, and seeing women who bear it easily may put pressure on others. With this statement, the star is supporting other moms who may be dealing with the same problems. But despite all these difficulties, Jenner is doing much better now.

Jenner is planning to have an even bigger family.

In 2020, on her Instagram Live, Jenner said that she wants 7 kids in total, but not now. The star agrees that pregnancy is serious business, and she is not ready to take this responsibility yet. But today, Jenner is on the right path with her 2 kids.

Do you think that it’s even harder to be a celeb mom? How was your motherhood experience?
