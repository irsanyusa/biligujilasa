Actor Victor Webster bio

The famous actor was born on 7, February 1973. His father was a police officer, and his mother was a hair stylist.

During his childhood days, Webster was a naughty child and was kicked out of schools several times. His parents could not bear with their troublesome son and were forced to send him to a martial arts school.

The move helped him discover his potential in kickboxing. He became a heavyweight black belt before claiming an undefeated record as an amateur kickboxer. His exceptional performance elevated him to a teacher in the school.

Webster developed an interest in acting but first had to work as a stockbroker, then in import and export business. He also served as a model for Versace, Armani, Calvin Klein and Valentino for a few years before pursuing his acting dreams.
